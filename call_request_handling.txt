Call Request Handling – Client

When a new SEM contract is received. Coordinators will generate tasks in advance and assigne them to OMA. These tasks sets are assigned on a turned base model.
OMA will plan the upcoming calls with MAC and send out invites to clients



Call Request Handling – Internal

OMA can make themselves available for internal calls with MAC.
The request needs to be made though a Salesforce case and case must contain the name of the OMA required (eg: “We’re having a launch call next week and I need an internal call with XYZ priori to that”).
Case is assigned to OMA who will follow up with MAC base on availability/bandwith.
